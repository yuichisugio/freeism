## Introduction

As a social mechanism, we have devised a system called "freeism."<br>
It is intended to be used as a mechanism to complement or replace capitalism.

## Overview

Below we present an overview of the freeism specification.<br>
Details of each item below are covered in subsequent chapters.

### What we want to achieve (in brief)

1. Improving working conditions that are not viable as businesses
2. Enabling diverse forms of evaluation
3. Accelerating the pace of technological innovation
